Monkey Trail

The Monkey Trail was built and designed in partnership with Chase Trails and features technical rock gardens, boardwalks, jumps and drops.

It is some serious fun and definitely some of the best riding in this part of the country.

The trail is primarily used for hiking, running, horses, and mountain biking and is accessible year-round.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND US
